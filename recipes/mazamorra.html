Mazamorra

Go back to the previous page

Description

Mazamorra is a traditional South American dish, often served as a dessert or a sweet porridge. It's made from various ingredients like corn, fruit, milk, and sweeteners.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg white corn
  • 2 liters of water (can be replaced with milk)
  • 12 tablespoons of sugar
  • Cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • Vanilla essence to taste
  • 2 liters of milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Soak the corn overnight before its preparation.

  2. Step 2

    3. The next day, boil the corn for approximately two hours in water and/or milk with the cinnamon, baking soda, vanilla essence, and sugar.

  3. Step 3

    4. Remove the "mazamorra" when the corn kernels are tender.
